Ads Performance Analysis

Key metrics I track to optimize campaign performance and maximize ROI

ROAS

Return on Ad Spend

Measures revenue generated for every dollar spent on ads. Target: 3:1 or higher to ensure profitable campaigns and sustainable growth.

Cost Per Message

CPM - Messaging Objective

Tracks cost efficiency for generating customer conversations. Lower CPM indicates better targeting and ad relevance for driving engagement.

Impressions

Total Ad Views

Total number of times ads were displayed. Monitors reach and brand visibility, essential for awareness campaigns and market penetration.

Frequency

Average Views Per Person

Average times each person sees the ad. Optimal range: 2-3. Higher frequency may indicate ad fatigue and requires creative refresh.

Link Clicks

CTR - Click Through Rate

Measures ad engagement and interest level. Higher CTR reflects compelling creative and strong call-to-action that resonates with audience.

Lead Conversion Rate

Conversion Efficiency

Percentage of clicks that convert into leads. Critical for evaluating landing page effectiveness and overall funnel performance optimization.
